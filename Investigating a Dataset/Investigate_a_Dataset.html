Introduction¶

This report examines TMDB Movie Dataset. Here, I am interested in finding the important features related with the voting criteria for a particular movie of a genre, which has been votes by spectators. This report anlyses the relationship between vote(average) of a movie with other features like popularity or revenue generated or the budget. I have also mentioned overview of the correlation between the variables in this report.

There are few questions that will be answered by the end of this report:

  1. What are the correaltions between the feature variables in this dataset?
  2. Is their any trend between the number of spectators voting for a partcular movie and revenue generated?
  3. Is their any trend between the number of spectators voting for a particular movie and the popularity of the movie?
  4. Whether a more popular movie will generate higher revenue or not?
  5. What is the length of the movies being released? Also, how is it different nowadays, in terms of the number of movies being released every year?

Below are the columns description of the dataset:¶

  • id - A unique identifier for each movie.
  • imdb_id - Imdb_id is also an identifier for each movie.
  • cast - The name of lead and supporting actors.
  • director - The name of Director.
  • budget - The budget in which the movie was made.
  • genre - The genre of the movie, Action, Comedy ,Thriller etc.
  • homepage - A link to the homepage of the movie.
  • keywords - The keywords or tags related to the movie.
  • original_title - The title of the movie before translation or adaptation.
  • overview - A brief description of the movie.
  • popularity - A numeric quantity specifying the movie popularity.
  • production_companies - The production house of the movie.
  • release_date - The date on which it was released.
  • revenue - The worldwide revenue generated by the movie.
  • runtime - The running time of the movie in minutes.
  • status - "Released" or "Rumored".
  • tagline - Movie's tagline.
  • vote_average - average ratings the movie recieved.
  • vote_count - the count of votes recieved.
